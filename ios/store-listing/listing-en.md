**Name:** COHRM - One Health Cameroon

**Subtitle:** One Health surveillance system

**Promotional Text:**
Report health rumors and events in Cameroon. Official application of the One Health Programme.

**Description:**

COHRM (Cameroon One Health Rumor Management) is the official application of Programme Zoonoses / One Health Cameroon for reporting, tracking, and managing health rumors and events across the entire Cameroonian territory.

Within the framework of the "One Health" approach, this application connects citizens, community health workers, veterinarians, and health authorities for effective and responsive epidemiological surveillance.

KEY FEATURES:

- Quick reporting with automatic geolocation and photos
- Public reporting without account creation
- Real-time dashboard and statistics
- Interactive maps of health events
- Real-time tracking of your reports
- Push notifications for health alerts
- Automatic rumor scanner on social media
- Multi-level validation and risk assessment
- Epidemiological reports and analytics
- Offline mode with automatic synchronization
- Bilingual support French / English

COVERAGE:
All 10 regions of Cameroon. Categories: human health, animal health, environment, food safety, natural disasters.

Developed by Programme Zoonoses / One Health Cameroon.
