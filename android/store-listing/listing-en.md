**Title:** COHRM - One Health Cameroon

**Short description:** Health rumor reporting and tracking in Cameroon

**Long description:**

COHRM (Cameroon One Health Rumor Management) is the official application of Programme Zoonoses / One Health Cameroon for reporting, tracking, and managing health rumors and events across the entire Cameroonian territory.

Within the framework of the "One Health" approach, this application connects citizens, community health workers, veterinarians, and health authorities for effective and responsive epidemiological surveillance.

KEY FEATURES:

- Quick Reporting: Submit a health report in a few steps with automatic geolocation, photos, and detailed description
- Public Reporting: Citizens can report events without creating an account, simply with a phone number
- SMS Reporting: Send a report via SMS in areas with low connectivity
- Real-time Tracking: Check the processing status of your reports at any time
- Dashboard: View surveillance statistics by region, category, status, and risk level
- Interactive Maps: Locate health events on a geographic map
- Notifications: Receive alerts about new rumors, validations, and updates
- Offline Mode: Save your reports as drafts and synchronize them when a connection is available
- Rumor Scanner: Automatically detect health rumors on social media and news sites (authorized users)
- Multi-level Validation: Participate in the verification and risk assessment process (authorized agents)
- Reports and Analytics: View performance reports and epidemiological trends

COVERAGE:
The application covers all 10 regions of Cameroon and supports the following categories: human health, animal health, environment, food safety, and natural disasters.
