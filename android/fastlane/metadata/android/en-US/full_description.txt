COHRM - One Health Rumor Management System Cameroon

The COHRM mobile application enables community health workers and health professionals to report, track, and manage health events in Cameroon using the One Health approach.

KEY FEATURES:

- Quick Reporting: Multi-step form for reporting health events (human, animal, environmental)
- GPS Location: Automatic geolocation of reports on the map
- Offline Mode: Works without internet connection, with automatic synchronization
- Dashboard: Real-time statistics and trends
- Rumor Management: 5-level validation cycle tracking
- Web Scanner: Automatic detection of health rumors online
- Notifications: Real-time alerts for critical events
- SMS: Structured SMS reporting for areas without internet

SECURITY:
- Secure JWT authentication
- Encryption of sensitive data
- Reporter confidentiality protection

Developed by the Ministry of Public Health of Cameroon as part of the One Health program.
